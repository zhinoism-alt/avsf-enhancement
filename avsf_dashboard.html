Quick Reference — EMEA Payment Methods (P&S / ADI)
PM 5
Domestic
Same country → same country in local currency. No IBAN required for domestic. F110: Posting date = Day +1
PM 6
No IBAN
Vendor has no IBAN. Works for any country / currency. Uses bank key + account number.
PM 7
Foreign / Non-SEPA
Non-SEPA countries · Non-EUR to SEPA · Foreign currency in domestic. IBAN required if vendor has one.
PM 8
SEPA EUR
EUR → SEPA zone bank. Requires IBAN + SWIFT. Will fail without both. F110: Posting date = Day +1
PM B
Swiss Domestic CHF
CH → CH in CHF only. Swiss-specific. F110: Posting date = Day +1
PM 3
FIK (Denmark)
Danish FIK invoices. Format: *71/000000000000000+000000<
PM 4
Giro / Split (SE·PL)
SE: Giro — no IBAN required. PL: Split payment — prework required.
PM 2
Foreign (CH) / HSBC MX
CH: Foreign payments. MX: HSBC-to-HSBC (bank 021) — use account number, NOT CLABE.

🇩🇪 Germany — Special Note
⚠️ Germany (DE) does not expose company details through VIES.
By law, Germany only confirms VAT number valid/invalid — no company name or address is returned. This is intentional under German privacy law (Datenschutz).

What to do for DE vendors:
  • Use VIES to confirm the DE VAT number is valid (format: DE + 9 digits)
  • Use Handelsregister to confirm company name and address
  • Check Bundesanzeiger for annual reports — may contain IBAN in financial statements
  • Ask the vendor directly for a VAT certificate (Umsatzsteuer-Identifikationsnummer Bescheinigung)
